SURFING


Surfing has gained a massive popularity in a short period of time in Sri Lanka, and it has made Sri Lanka a widely known tropical surf paradise, And it is a perfect place to begin your surfing journey. Weligama, Mirissa etc. are widely known for surfing. It is a must experience activity for the ones who love beach areas.

SAFARI

Your trip isn’t completed if you didn’t go for a safari in Sri Lanka. It is one of the best things to do in Sri Lanka. There are 22 national parks which has safari in Sri Lanka, and it definitely is hard to chose one when you have so many choices. You can admire so many different varieties of animals when you go for a safari, Wild elephants, Leopards, Tigers etc.

HIKING


Sri Lanka is a significant place for hiking. It has not only walks but also there are intensive and thrilling walks on the mountains. There are so many popular hiking areas in Sri Lanka as,

  • Ella Rock
  • Adams Peak
  • Pidurangala etc.

They are one of the most amazing experiences in Sri Lanka.
