BEACHES IN SRI LANKA

The beautiful country Sri Lanka consists full of natural beauty. Beaches are one of the reason why Sri Lanka is famous for, people from around the world visits Sri Lanka to explore them. They make sure you revisit to explore them. Also, beaches makes you feel relaxed, Sri Lankan beaches are ideal places for you to recharge completely.

PASIKUDAH


Pasikuda is located in Eastern seaboard in Sri Lanka. Their beaches attracts many people. It is widely famous for its white sand beaches and shaded trees. You can visit this place any time in the year. And also it is safe for both beginners and experts to swim there. Click here to book a hotel at Pasikudah

MIRISSA


Mirissa is also one of the popular beaches in Sri Lanka. It is located in the southern Sri Lanka. The best time to visit is December to March. You can spot Whales and Dolphin. Also you can see turtles hatching if you visit in December. It is also a must visit place for hodophiles. Click here to book a villa at mirissa

ARUGAM-BAY


Arugam Bay is located in the east coast of Sri Lanka. It is a gem of a place in Sri Lanka. It is one of the popular tourist spots in Sri Lanka. Best time to visit is March to October. It is also one of most famous surfing spot in the world, you can rent basic equipment from the local shops. It is a must visit place for everyone. Click here to book a hotel in ArugamBay
