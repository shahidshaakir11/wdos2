KANDY



Kandy is one of the sacred places in Sri Lanka. It is the last royal kingdom of Sri Lanka. It is surrounded by beautiful mountains. It is located in the central province of Sri Lanka. Kandy is well known for the tooth relic (dalada maligawa).


During August Kandy becomes very crowded because of the Esala Perahera which is an annual tradition of Temple of The Sacred Tooth. It also attracts a huge number of tourists. The parade includes around 50 elephants with colorful Kandyan dancers.


There is so many things to talk about beauty in/around Kandy, you can discover so many spectacular mountains, beautiful lakes etc. around Kandy. Kandy is a must visit place as a tourist destination.
