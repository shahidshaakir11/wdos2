WELCOME TO SRI LANKA

Sri Lanka is an island in the Indian ocean. This country is widely known for tourist attraction and tea plantation. Sri Lanka was earlier known as Ceylon. Also, Sri Lanka is famous for the spices they export around the world, they are one of the best in the world. And Sri Lanka also has a beautiful history, to reminisce them it is a msut to visit this island.

RELIGIONS IN SRI LANKA


Buddhism is the main religion in Sri Lanka and is mostly common in Southern part of Sri Lanka Hinduism is a main religion in the nothern part of Sri Lanka. Islam is common in East part of Sri Lanka. Christianity is mostly performed in Gampaha and in puttalam.

LANGUAGES IN SRI LANKA


Sinhala and Tamil are the most dominant languages in Sri Lanka. Sinhala is the language of the Sinhalese who are residing within Sri Lanka. Tamil is mostly used in the nothern part of Sri Lanka.

SPICES IN SRI LANKA


Spices are another thing for which Sri Lanka is famous for. There are exquisite range of spices for centuries in Sri Lanka. It is these spices that are behind the delicious flavors of Sri Lankan dishes, be it any kind of a dish.
