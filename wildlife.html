UDAWALAWE NATIONAL PARK


Surrounded by the central mountains, Udawalawe is an ideal place to see wild elephants, it will definitely be an amazing sight to see elephants roaming in herds. Not only udawalawe is beautiful by elephants, but there many other animals such like buffalos, monkeys, crocodiles, varities of birds and etc. Udawalawe is easily one of the best tourist attraction spot in Sri Lanka.

YALA NATIONAL PARK


Yala national park is the most famous national park in Sri Lanka, this national park is home to more than 200 bird species and more than 40 mammal species. You can expect to see elephants roaming, herds of deers and many other animals there Also, not to forget this park has the highest concentration of leopards in the world. This is one perfect tourist attraction destination.

SINHARAJA FOREST RESERVE

Sinharaja forest reserve the biggest rainforest in Sri Lanka which is also a world heritage listed, is a biodiversity hotspot. Sinharaja is home to more than half of the endemic species of mammals in Sri Lanka.
